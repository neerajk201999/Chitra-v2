# Chitra · Video Workflows

Select one recipe, then run `create-video` from intake through release. This
skill chooses format and beat structure; it never bypasses Direction, gates,
rendered evidence, or critique.

## Product launch

Use `brand-film` or `product-demo`, normally 30–45 seconds. Structure: cold
open from approved positioning → problem → turn → product proof with real UI →
close. Acquire real UI before scoring. Copy comes from approved product truth,
not invented claims. Use a music bed and sparse SFX under MO-AUD-3.

## Screen demo

Use `product-demo`, normally 45–90 seconds. Start from a screen recording and
optional narration. When speech or clip audio matters, follow create-video's
`footage-editing.md`: lock the provider-neutral word transcript, read the compact
phrase pack, approve an explainable EDL, and render a source-audio-preserving
plate. Select 3–6 timestamped proof moments, use
moving clips for interaction and stills for holds, and crop recorder chrome,
personal data, toolbars, and transient toasts before scoring. Captions describe
what the footage actually proves.

## Social short

Use `social-short`, normally 9:16 at 1080×1920 for 8–20 seconds. Keep one idea:
hook within two seconds → one proof beat → mark. Use the register type minimums,
land cuts on a declared beat grid, and keep every scene under four seconds.

If the brief does not fit one recipe, use `create-video` directly and state the
chosen register and structure in Direction rather than forcing a template.
